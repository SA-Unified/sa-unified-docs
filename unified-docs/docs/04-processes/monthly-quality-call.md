## Description
A mandatory monthly outreach call to every patient to evaluate service satisfaction and identify any significant changes in their health status.

---
## Protocol

### 1. The Call

Contact the patient or their legally authorized representative directly via telephone.

### 2. Question Checklist

During the conversation, verify the following key metrics:

- **Caregiver Performance:** Are you satisfied with the services provided by your current caregiver?
- **Health Status:** Have there been any noticeable changes in your general health?
- **Safety Incidents:** Have there been any falls, emergency room visits, or hospitalizations since our last call?

### 3. Documentation (In HHAX)

Record the results of the call immediately within **[[HHAeXchange]]**.

- **Action:** Create a note in the patient’s profile.
- **IMPORTANT:** Select the note type **"Internal Note: YES"**.
    - *Reason:* This is an internal quality audit. Unless a specific problem requiring payer intervention is found, the insurance company (Plan) does not need to see routine wellness check logs.

### 4. Escalation (Optional)

If the patient expresses dissatisfaction or indicates they are considering leaving the agency—**notify the Supervisor immediately.**
