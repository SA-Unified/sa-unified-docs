## Description

The administrative workflow for a caregiver to receive their benefit card, used for transit costs and incidental expenses.

## Timeline (Managing Expectations)

Caregivers frequently complain about delays. It is the Coordinator's job to clearly explain the **60-day lag**:

1. **Month 1:** The caregiver works their initial shifts.
2. **Month 2 (Beginning):** The total hours worked are calculated and transmitted to the third-party provider, **FBA**.
3. **Month 2 (Mid-Month):** FBA processes the data, issues the physical card, and sends it via USPS.
    - **The Reality:** A new caregiver will likely not see their card until **2 full months** after their start date.

---

## Troubleshooting

If a caregiver asks, "Where is my card?":

- **Scenario A: Worked < 2 months:**
    - *Action:* Explain the 60-day processing cycle. Ask them to wait.
- **Scenario B: Worked > 2 months:**
    - *Action:* Direct the caregiver to contact the **[[Benefit Team]]** directly for a status update or tracking number.

> [!NOTE] **Replenishment Cycle**
Once the card is active, funds are replenished on the **15th of every month** for the hours worked in the previous month.
