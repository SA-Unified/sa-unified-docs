## Definition
The Attorney General (AG) is the head of NY's Department of Law and acts as both the People's Lawyer and the state's chief legal officer. The AG serves as the guardian of the legal rights of the people of New York, its organizations, and its natural resources. 

Simply, the AG enforces laws to protect consumers, tenants, patients, workers, investors, and charitable donors.
