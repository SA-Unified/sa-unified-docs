## Definitions 

Activities of Daily Living (ADLs) are tasks we do to stay alive and well. These important tasks tend to be done on a regular basis to take care of your body and overall being. 

Examples include: 
- Eating
- Going to the bathroom
- Bathing
- Money Management
- Cooking
- Laundry

The ability to perform ADLs is an essential measurement of an individual's functional status, where the inability to do so may lead to unsafe conditions and a poor quality of life.
